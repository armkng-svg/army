The Art of Palmistry

Palmistry, also known as chiromancy, is the practice of interpreting the lines and shapes of the hand to understand personality and possible life paths. It has been practiced in many cultures around the world.

Main Lines of the Hand

There are three major lines often studied in palmistry: the Heart Line, the Head Line, and the Life Line. Each line is believed to represent different aspects of a person's character and experiences.

Mounts of the Palm

The raised areas of the palm are called mounts. Each mount is named after a planet and is said to influence certain traits such as ambition, creativity, or emotional strength.

Is Palmistry Scientific?

Palmistry is considered a traditional or cultural practice rather than a science. Many people see it as a symbolic tool for reflection rather than a method of prediction.
